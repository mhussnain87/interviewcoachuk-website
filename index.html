FAQ

Common questions

Is there a free trial?

Yes — Premium comes with a 3-day free trial on monthly and annual plans. Cancel anytime before the trial ends and you won't be charged.

Can it prepare me for a specific job advert?

Yes — paste the full job description into the app and it generates a tailored interview pack for that exact role: 12 likely questions, the competencies the panel will probe hardest (with evidence quoted from the advert), and tactical preparation insights.

Is the app really free to download?

Yes — Interview Coach UK is free to download on iOS and Android. You get 30 Universal competency questions, mock interview mode, and basic AI feedback at no cost. Premium unlocks all 315 questions across 9 sectors.

What is the STAR method?

STAR stands for Situation, Task, Action, Result. It's the most widely used framework for answering competency-based interview questions in the UK. The app teaches you the method and gives you feedback on how well you're applying it.

Which sectors are covered?

NHS, Civil Service, Technology, Finance, Education, Retail, Engineering, Marketing, and Universal (suitable for any role).

Can I cancel my subscription anytime?

Yes. Subscriptions are managed through the App Store or Google Play and can be cancelled anytime from your device settings. You keep access until the end of your billing period.
