FAQ

Common questions

Is the app really free to download?

Yes — Interview Coach UK is free to download on iOS. You get immediate access to 30 Universal competency questions, mock interview mode, and basic AI feedback at no cost. Premium unlocks all 315 questions across 9 sectors.

What is the STAR method?

STAR stands for Situation, Task, Action, Result. It's the most widely used framework for answering competency-based interview questions in the UK. Our app teaches you the method and gives you feedback on how well you're applying it.

Which sectors are covered?

We cover NHS, Civil Service, Technology, Finance, Education, Retail, Engineering, Marketing, and Universal (suitable for any role). Each sector pack contains 35 tailored questions.

Can I cancel my subscription anytime?

Yes. Subscriptions are managed through Apple and can be cancelled at any time from your iPhone settings. You'll keep access until the end of your billing period.

Is this app available on Android?

Currently iOS only. An Android version is in development and coming soon. Sign up to be notified when it launches.

How is the AI feedback generated?

We use advanced AI to analyse your answers against best-practice STAR criteria. Feedback is specific to your answer — not generic — and includes what you did well and concrete suggestions to improve.
