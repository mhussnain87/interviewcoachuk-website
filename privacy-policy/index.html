Privacy Policy

Last updated: 4 July 2026

1. Introduction

Interview Coach UK ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your data when you use the Interview Coach UK mobile application and website (interviewcoachuk.com).

2. Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of information:

  • Account information: email address, name, and authentication data when you create an account.
  • Usage data: information about how you use the app, including questions practised, sessions completed, and features accessed.
  • Device information: device type, operating system, and push notification tokens for sending reminders.
  • Payment information: subscription purchases are processed by Apple App Store or Google Play. We do not store your payment card details.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Provide and improve the Interview Coach UK app and services.
  • Send smart practice reminders (if you have enabled notifications).
  • Process subscription purchases and manage your account.
  • Respond to support requests.
  • Analyse usage patterns to improve the app experience.

4. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored securely using Google Firebase, which complies with industry-standard security practices. We implement appropriate technical and organisational measures to protect your personal information against unauthorised access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction.

5. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • Firebase (Google): authentication, database, and push notifications.
  • RevenueCat: subscription management.
  • OpenAI: AI-powered feedback and coaching features.
  • Anthropic: AI-powered sample answer generation.
  • AppsFlyer: app install attribution and marketing analytics. AppsFlyer collects your device identifier (Apple IDFA or Android Advertising ID, only if you consent) and information about how you found and installed the app, to help us understand which marketing channels bring users to Interview Coach UK.
  • Apple App Store / Google Play: app distribution and payment processing.

Managing tracking preferences: On iOS, when you first open the app you will see an App Tracking Transparency prompt asking whether you allow tracking. You can decline, and you can change this later in Settings → Privacy & Security → Tracking. On Android, you can reset or opt out of your Advertising ID in Settings → Google → Ads. If you opt out, AppsFlyer will not collect your advertising identifier, though anonymous install counts may still be recorded.

For more information on how AppsFlyer processes data, see their privacy policy at https://www.appsflyer.com/legal/services-privacy-policy/.

6. Data Retention

We retain your personal data for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide services. You may request deletion of your account and associated data at any time by contacting us or using the "Delete Account" feature within the app.

7. Your Rights

Under UK GDPR, you have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you.
  • Request correction of inaccurate data.
  • Request deletion of your data.
  • Object to or restrict processing of your data.
  • Data portability.

8. Children's Privacy

Interview Coach UK is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any significant changes by updating the date at the top of this page.

10. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at: info@interviewcoachuk.com
